I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me.

I evoke the term 'handmade web' to refer to

web pages coded by hand rather than by software;

web pages made and maintained by individuals

rather than by businesses or corporations;

web pages which are provisional, temporary,

or one-of-a-kind; web pages which challenge

conventions of reading, writing, design, ownership,

privacy, security, or identity.
